Frequently Asked Questions


  • How long will it be before you pair me with a Nanny?

    We pair you with a nanny rather quickly! Once you contact us, we’ll consult with you to determine your needs. From there we conduct and perform a thorough search to find your ideal nanny. Will set up interviews for you to get to know the perspective candidates. This process typically takes anywhere between 10 to 25 days, all depending on any special qualifications that you would like your nanny to have. But if you require a nanny in short notice, we can always accommodate!

  • How exactly does your Sleep Training process work?

    We’ll start off by having you complete a detailed family history questionnaire before scheduling either an in-person consultation or a video-chat consultation. From there, we’ll answer any questions that you may have had about the questionnaire, and we’ll conduct an in-home consultation at your home . After this step, our team will complete a comprehensive customized sleep training plan designed specifically for your child. This plan will help you learn exactly what you can do as a parent to ensure that your child receives the full night’s sleep that he or she deserves. Lastly, we provide ongoing support with unlimited phone and email support for as long as three weeks after the implementation of your sleep plan. (On-going consultation available if needed.)

  • How do you screen for Nannies?

    Our nannies are only the most experienced, professional, and reputable nannies found in your local area. Legacy conducts phone and video-chat interviews with several candidates. Then we conduct a criminal background check, verify their information, their experience, and even their state-issued ID and CPR training certification to ensure that they’re truly the best of the best.

  • How do your Nannies get paid?

    Once Legacy finds your ideal nanny you will pay them based upon the agreed salary. We find nannies that will work for your desired rate.

  • If I’m unsatisfied with my service, can I contact Legacy?

    Of course! Should you ever be unsatisfied with any of the services that we or the nannies provide, we encourage that you contact us directly. We could be incredibly flexible in ensuring that you’re able to take advantage of each of our services, from nannying, to sleep training, to date night sitting.
